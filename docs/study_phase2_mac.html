DCF, EDCA AC Queues, CWmin/CWmax/AIFSN per AC

Prereq: Phase 1 Day 1–2 (OFDM, channel contention basics)
Socratic Questions
Voice AC (AC_VO) and Video AC (AC_VI) both expire their backoff timers at the same slot. Why does AC_VO win — and what parameter specifically determines the tie-break?
CWmin for AC_VO is 3 slots vs 15 slots for AC_BE. If 10 clients all transmit simultaneously, why does the collision probability for voice still increase — and what protects it?
Reading (T1 Corpus)

CWNP - 802-11_arbitration.pdf
80211-2024.pdf §10 — EDCA parameter sets, TXOP limits

Lab Exercise
EDCA TXOP Burst Capture
Generate voice traffic (FaceTime or iPerf3 UDP small packets) from iPhone → AP1 capture → Wireshark filter wlan.qos.tid == 6 (AC_VO) → measure TXOP burst duration → compare AC_VO TXOP vs AC_BE TXOP from the same capture.

NAV, RTS/CTS Protection, MU-RTS, BSS Color Spatial Reuse

Prereq: Day 1 (EDCA, slot timing)
Socratic Questions
Two BSSes have different BSS Colors. Client A in BSS-1 hears a frame from BSS-2 at −74 dBm; OBSS_PD threshold is −72 dBm. Does Client A defer or transmit — and which specific field in the HE PPDU header makes that decision?
RTS/CTS protection was designed for hidden-node. MU-RTS is its 802.11ax successor. What does MU-RTS add that classic RTS can't do in a multi-client DL MU scenario?

80211be-2024.pdf — BSS Color, OBSS_PD, spatial reuse parameter set

Lab Exercise
BSS Color Beacon Verification
Capture beacons on AP1 → decode HE Operation IE → record BSS Color value → change BSS Color in XIQ → capture again → verify IE updates → if a neighbor BSS is visible, compare colors.

Day 3 of 6

Aggregation — A-MPDU vs A-MSDU, BlockAck Windows

Prereq: Day 1–2
Socratic Questions
A-MSDU aggregates before encryption; A-MPDU aggregates after. Why does this order matter for retransmission granularity — and which one do you want in a lossy RF environment?
The BlockAck window size is 64 frames in 11n and 256 in 11ax. If one frame is lost in a 256-frame window, what's the minimum retransmission overhead — and why doesn't the sender retransmit all 256?
Reading (T1 Corpus)

Aruba VHD Config PDF (T2) — aggregation policy
80211-2024.pdf §10.3 — A-MPDU, A-MSDU, BA

Lab Exercise
BlockAck Frame Decode
AP1 capture during iPerf3 bulk transfer → Wireshark filter wlan.fc.type_subtype == 0x0019 (Block Ack) → expand BA bitmap → count subframe count in preceding A-MPDU → verify bitmap length matches window size.

TWT — Individual, Broadcast, Restricted

Prereq: Day 1 (EDCA, TXOP concepts)
Socratic Questions
A printer negotiates individual TWT with a 60-second sleep interval and 100 ms service period. What's the worst-case latency for a print job that arrives 1 ms after the service period closes — and how does the AP handle queued frames during the sleep?
Broadcast TWT lets the AP schedule all clients simultaneously. What's the downside for dense deployments where clients have heterogeneous traffic patterns?

Multicast/Broadcast over Wi-Fi — DTIM Cost, ARP/ND, IGMP Snooping Boundary

Prereq: Day 5 (DTIM, buffered frames)
Socratic Questions
IGMP snooping on SW1 limits which switch ports receive multicast streams. Why does enabling it not reduce Wi-Fi airtime by default — and where exactly does the multicast-to-unicast conversion have to happen?
Every ARP broadcast is sent at the lowest mandatory rate (typically 6 Mbps). If you have 50 clients and 5 ARP broadcasts per second per client, what's the airtime cost — and what modern protocol eliminates this?
