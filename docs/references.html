A. CWNA-108 Chapter Map

Each curriculum day points at the CWNA-108 chapter topic that covers the same concept at exam-objective depth. Use CWNA-108 as the baseline; this curriculum goes one layer deeper into PHY/MAC mechanics and adds Wi-Fi 7 + EP1 context.

Verify chapter numbers in your edition. Sybex CWNA-108 (Westcott & Coleman, 2020) chapter numbering shifted slightly between print and Kindle editions. Use the chapter topic name as the authoritative pointer.
