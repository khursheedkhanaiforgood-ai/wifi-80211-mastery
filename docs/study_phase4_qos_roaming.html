Voice Roaming Budget — 50 ms Target, 11r vs OKC Cost

Prereq: Phase 3 Day 1 (FT/OKC keying hierarchy)
Socratic Questions
The G.114 voice quality threshold requires end-to-end latency under 150 ms; the roaming contribution budget is often set at 50 ms. A full 802.11i 4-way handshake takes ~80–120 ms in a loaded cell. What specific steps does 11r eliminate — and what's the residual cost?
OKC avoids the full 802.1X re-authentication but still requires a 4-way handshake. Why does OKC still take ~40–60 ms while FT is ~15–20 ms — what's the extra round-trip in OKC that FT avoids?
